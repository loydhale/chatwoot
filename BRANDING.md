# SupportFlow Branding Guide

## Brand Names

| Original | New Name | Context |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Chatwoot | DeskFlow | Product name |
| chatwoot | deskflow | lowercase/code |
| Captain | Atlas | AI Assistant |
| captain | atlas | lowercase/code |

## Why These Names

### DeskFlow
- Clean, professional
- Implies workflow automation
- Easy to say and remember
- Works well for agencies

### Atlas
- Strong, reliable (like the Greek Titan)
- Conveys support and foundation
- Friendly but professional
- Different enough from competitors (Copilot, Assistant, etc.)
